# Transit Forecast — Interpretation Guide

Reference for transit significance ranking, orb thresholds, duration estimates, and aspect interpretation in a transit context. Load on demand during Phase 3.

---

## Transit significance ranking

Use this to select and order the Major Transits section. Higher tier = more significant.

### Tier 1 — Life-defining

Outer planets transiting personal planets or angles. These are the defining influences of a period.

| Transiting | Aspecting natal | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| Pluto | Sun, Moon, Ascendant, Midheaven | Profound transformation; identity-level change |
| Neptune | Sun, Moon, Venus, Ascendant | Dissolution, spirituality, confusion, or idealism |
| Uranus | Sun, Moon, Ascendant, Mars | Disruption, liberation, sudden change |
| Saturn | Sun, Moon, Ascendant, Venus, Mars | Structure, discipline, karma, earned mastery |

### Tier 2 — Medium-term shaping

Outer planets to outer natal planets, or Jupiter to personal planets.

| Transiting | Aspecting natal | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| Pluto | Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn | Intensification of the natal planet's domain |
| Neptune | Mercury, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn | Confusion or inspiration in the natal domain |
| Uranus | Mercury, Venus, Jupiter, Saturn | Sudden shifts in how the natal planet expresses |
| Saturn | Mercury, Jupiter, Uranus, Neptune, Pluto | Testing and structuring those domains |
| Jupiter | Sun, Moon, Ascendant, Mercury, Venus, Mars | Expansion, opportunity, optimism |

### Tier 3 — Shorter-cycle influence

Inner planets transiting natal positions. Noticeable but brief.

| Transiting | Aspecting natal | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| Mars | Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Ascendant | Activation, assertiveness, conflict trigger |
| Venus | Sun, Moon, Jupiter, Ascendant | Ease, pleasure, relationship connection |
| Mercury | Sun, Moon, Jupiter, Saturn | Communication, decisions, information |
| Sun | Sun, Moon, Ascendant | Annual renewal point; clarity on the natal planet |

Within each tier, conjunctions and oppositions outrank squares; squares outrank trines and sextiles.

## Orb thresholds for transits

Transits use tighter orbs than natal aspects. The influence is felt as the transiting planet approaches (applying) and dissipates as it separates.

| Aspect | Applying orb | Separating orb |
|---|---|---|
| Conjunction | 5° | 3° |
| Opposition | 5° | 3° |
| Square | 4° | 2° |
| Trine | 4° | 2° |
| Sextile | 3° | 1° |

**Applying vs. separating:** When the transiting planet is approaching exact (applying), the transit's energy is building — often felt as anticipation, pressure, or accumulating change. When separating, the peak has passed and the energy is integrating or resolving.

## Transit duration guide

| Transiting planet | Typical transit duration | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Pluto | 1–3 years | Retrograde typically produces 3 exact hits over 1–2 years |
| Neptune | 1–2 years | Slow station; retrograde often produces 3 hits |
| Uranus | 6–18 months | Station-heavy; 3 hits common; sudden and unpredictable |
| Saturn | 3–6 months | Often 3 hits due to retrograde; intensifies at station |
| Jupiter | 1–3 months | Faster orbit; often single exact hit |
| Mars | 1–3 weeks | 2–3 months if retrograde (rare) |
| Sun | 1–3 days | — |
| Mercury | 1–5 days | Weeks if retrograde and stationing near natal planet |
| Venus | 3–7 days | Weeks if retrograde and stationing |

**Station intensification:** When a slow planet (Saturn or outer) stations within 1° of a natal planet, the transit is dramatically extended and intensified — it can feel as if the natal planet is "held" in that energy for weeks. Always flag station proximity explicitly.

## Multi-hit transits

When an outer planet retrogrades back over a natal planet, the transit hits exact three times:

1. **First hit (direct)** — The theme enters awareness; initial events or encounters
2. **Retrograde period** — Review, revision, and deepening; the theme becomes internal
3. **Third hit (direct again)** — Resolution, integration, or culmination

Note all three dates when known. Flag the retrograde period between hits 1 and 3 as a time of reflection and internal processing. The full arc from hit 1 to hit 3 is the true duration of this transit's story.

## Aspect interpretation in transit context

Natal aspects describe character. Transit aspects describe timing — they activate the natal planet's energy through the lens of the transiting planet and the aspect type.

| Aspect | Transit meaning |
|---|---|
| Conjunction | The transiting planet merges with and amplifies the natal planet — a new cycle begins in that planet's domain |
| Opposition | Tension between the transiting energy and the natal planet; awareness often comes through external events or other people |
| Square | Friction and pressure that forces action or change; the most growth-intensive aspect |
| Trine | Natural flow and support; ease and opportunity in the natal planet's domain; requires intention to fully use |
| Sextile | Gentle opportunity requiring some effort to activate; cooperative and productive when engaged |
